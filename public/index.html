Gannon Building & Remolding


Since 1984 Gannon ahs been involved in residential and commercial rehabiliation and new construction. Job sites have ranged from Toms River to New York but Gannon building has primarily been in Monmouth County. Gannon building has specialized in rehabilitation of structures aged over one hundred years in the Ocrean Grove Area. Salvaging, relocating old wood structures and bringing them up to code standards has been a staple of Gannon building.
